Delete Your Inner Map Account

You can permanently delete your Inner Map account and all associated data at any time.

How to delete your account

From within the app (fastest):

  • Open Inner Map
  • Tap the menu icon (top-left)
  • Tap Settings
  • Scroll to Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion

By email:

If you no longer have the app installed, you can request deletion by emailing support@my-inner-map.com with the subject line "Delete my account." Please include any identifying information you remember (the email you used to sign in, if applicable).

Email requests are processed within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following is permanently removed:

  • Your inner map (wound, parts, beliefs, body locations)
  • All chat history and AI conversations
  • All journal entries (text and voice)
  • Session summaries and progress data
  • Partner connections and shared conversations
  • Sign-in identity (Apple, Google, or email — if linked)
  • Your account record

What may be retained

Anonymized usage logs (no personal content, no identifiers tied to you) may be retained for up to 90 days for security and fraud prevention purposes. After 90 days, all logs are deleted automatically.
